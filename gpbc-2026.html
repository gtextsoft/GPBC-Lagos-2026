About the Conference

A focused gathering for serious real estate professionals.

The Global Property Brokers Conference 2026 brings brokers, investors and industry leaders together for a single, high-value day in Lagos.

Hosted by the Stephen Akintayo Foundation and convened by Dr. Stephen Akintayo, the Lagos Edition is designed for professionals who want to grow their business, expand their network and close meaningful deals.

Across one intensive day, attendees gain direct insight from global and local authorities in real estate, connect with credible peers and partners, and leave with practical strategies they can apply immediately.

01One-day intensive

A concentrated programme designed to deliver maximum value within a single, well-structured day.

02Global & local expertise

Insight from internationally recognised figures alongside a proven leader in the African market.

03In-person in Lagos

A physical event built for genuine connection, conversation and deal-making.
